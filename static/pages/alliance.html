What is the Alternate Chan Alliance (ACA)?


The Alternate Chan Alliance is a group of imageboards based on the idea of free speech.
The Alternate Chan Alliance provides a good and secure posting environment so you can shitpost all you need without the worry of being censored over some retarded shit.

Keep in mind that just because the Alternate Chan Alliance promotes free speech, doesn't mean we allow or encourage anything illegal to be posted. All imageboards in the Alternate Chan Alliance are required to have some rules against illegal content.
